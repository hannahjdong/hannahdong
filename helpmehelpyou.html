Help Me Help You

For my second ever hackathon, I created a team with a group of amazing women. Our slogan was "no matter who you are: a business owner, a single parent or a senior citizen - you can post your need and find a friend in deed." The goal of our application was to create an online ecosystem for neighbors to come together and help each other out. When thinking through designs, we felt like an online bulletin board would fit our wide demographic. With real world references, it would be easier for older users to recognize the main features. Users would be able to post requests for their neighbors to complete. Check out the interactive prototype here!
