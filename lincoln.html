Lincoln Private Jets

This summer I worked as a UI/UX designer for the start-up Lincoln Private Jets. My main task was designing the mobile application and I worked closely with the front-end web developers. I attended research and development stand-ups three times a week and had many one-on-one meetings with the CEO and CFO. After accomplishing my main task, I redesigned the website with guidance from the team.

During my internship, I worked closely with the CEO to realize his vision. I reworked designs from the previous designer as shown in the images above. For the main booking page, I added relevant information while keeping the design clean and simple. I simplified the "Flight Results" page by removing redundant information and irrelevant tags that cluttered the space. For the "FlightPool" pages, I introduced users to the feature and designed a flight board for users to select flights in one click. Overall, my redesigns keep the integrity of the originals while improving the usability and efficiency for users.
